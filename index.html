ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Chevy McQuaide Jr., I’m 20 years old and I was born and raised in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I like to consider myself a jack of all trades, or at least I’m aiming to; though at the current moment I’m focused on an IT and music career, with art & poetry on the side. I’ve been in IT for two years, and even studied it as a trade in high school, so I’ve got 5 total years of IT on my hands. I’ve always been infatuated with technology and how it works, although it wasn’t until recently I studied how to tear things apart. My interest in technology spiked in video games and consoles, where i loved learning how concepts were made and implemented. I never saw myself making video games, but I’ve always loved the idea of maybe drawing characters and composing the soundtrack. Speaking of art and music, all my life revolves around it, as I’ve always considered art to be an important aspect of human life. Whether it be some fantasy painting, dark poetry, or some broad music, art is constantly revolving around in my head. I’ve been in a few bands here and there, and even in the process of recording and releasing an album with friends (I even drew part of the album cover). I play a lot of stringed instruments, but the guitar and singing are what I most excel in. I’m always out in the rock n roll scenes, supporting new and upcoming bands, and giving the underground the praise it deserves.

Currently in my life I’m with UTP and work in an elementary school, Farrell Elementary, where i manage every piece of technology there. Those include around 300-400 laptops, 100-200 tablets and around 200 desktops, and something is always messing up because of a teacher or student. I’m content and proud with what I currently do, but eventually I’d like to work my way up into a bigger building with more to manage, as I like being kept busy. In the music industry, as said before, I just recently joined a heavy metal band as their vocalist, and previously designed logos and merchandise for them. We’re already in the process of recording an album, which should be out before winter, and I’m pretty hyped to promote new music that I helped create. All in all, my life revolves around technology and art, and I’m happy with that.
